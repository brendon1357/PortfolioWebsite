Reaction Speed Game

Application Information

This game was made using Python and incroporates key programming fundamentals such as conditional statements, loops, arrays, mathematical expressions, and boolean statements. The objective of the game is to test your reaction and judgement speed to avoid two alternating red lasers and any of the falling blue squares. Your score is based on how many waves you survive and this number is stored at the top right of your screen while playing the game. You can restart the game by pressing "R" at any time, and you can also pause the game at any time by pressing "F" and then "C" to resume. Try to survive as many waves as you can.

How To Run
